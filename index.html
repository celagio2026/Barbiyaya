Why Barbiyaya?

A Therapy Club

You might be wondering: out of all the clubs out there, why should we choose Barbiyaya? Here in Barbiyaya, you have the ability to openly express your emotions without any inhibitions. You will have the chance to engage in heartfelt conversations with your fellow scholars; they might be experiencing the same thing as you are. There is no need to fret because every session offered here is complimentary, or free of charge.

What's Barbiyaya?

A Therapy Club

As fellow scholars, we understand how difficult it may be to find a sympathetic ear whenever you need it. So, we made the decision to create a club that aims to address that certain issue. Our club, Barbiyaya, is a place where people can openly express their emotions without inhibition or fear. We aim to make our club a safe space for everybody, so we guarantee that everything we talk about in the club stays just between us. We also aim to aid those who are having trouble expressing themselves and their emotions. You can decide whether to participate in group therapy sessions through Barbiyaya with your closest friends, future friends, or even just by yourself.
